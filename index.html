COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Finding the right care starts with having the right information.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain a maximum ratio of 8:1 for preschoolers and 4:1 for toddlers to ensure personalized attention and safety.

Are meals included in the tuition?

Yes, we provide nutritionist-approved breakfast, lunch, and afternoon snacks daily. All meals are prepared on-site following strict hygiene standards.

How do you handle medical emergencies?

All staff are CPR and First Aid certified. We have immediate access to local medical facilities and follow rigorous emergency response protocols.

What are your security protocols?

Our facility features 24/7 CCTV monitoring, controlled access entry systems, and a strict child release protocol requiring authorized adult identification.
